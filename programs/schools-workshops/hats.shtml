Hands-on Advance Tutorial Session at the LPC - HATS@LPC

The following schedule of HATS@LPC has been created. Details on each workshop, including an Indico page and more details about the technique, will be announced as the workshops get closer. All sessions will take place in WH11 Sunrise at the LPC. There will, of course, be limited video connections available for those who wish to connect from outside Fermilab. To take full advantage of these advance tutorials, we strongly encourage the participants to be present at the LPC. Advanced (no cost) registration will be requested for participation in these events.
