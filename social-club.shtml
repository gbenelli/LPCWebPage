LPC Social Club

From time to time, the LPC convenes informally at a tavern or a restauant near Fermilab, as the LPC Social Club. Downtime together with colleagues, both permanent and visting, can be surprisingly productive, and certainly restorative. In casual settings, that latest project may reveal itself to be someone else's solved problem. The nagging inconsistency seen in physics analysis, mentioned over a friendly drink, may just turn out to be well explained... or the beginning of an exciting discovery.

For more information contact stjohn@buphy.bu.edu
